This time, "We hack the crisis"



The Reality

This year, we are witnessing an economic crisis escalate into a humanitarian crisis. Hundreds of thousands are fleeing the war in the Middle East in search of a better life, only to find different obstacles in Europe. Many of the countries they travel through are facing a severe economic crisis, making it difficult to transfer money or receive donations. In Greece, capital controls restrict the free movement of capital through the traditional banking system, leaving the unbanked to rely on expensive alternatives or risk carrying large amounts of cash with them through their dangerous journey.


The Vision

We decided to move forward in organizing a local event in order to bring together people who can assist in providing solutions to these problems. Our mission is to assist people with the transfer of value during economic and geopolitical crises. Our mission is to create a series of events that will result in actionable solutions for the above problems locally and globally.


The Challenge

We "Hack the Crisis" by providing solutions to global problems with the use of the Bitcoin and the Blockchain Technology in order to assist solving the fundamental problems and tackle the problem of Value Transfer during socioeconomic crises.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Everything, including food, will be provided for free!

Can I Already Start Thinking, Or Even Working On My Hack?

We encourage all the Hackers of the Hackathon to already start brainstorming of what you will want to create and present during the Hackathon. You can additionally also start thinking of the languages you'll be using and the overall environment setup. But it is against the rules to start working on your Hack now, since it will be unfair for all the other Hackers. Be fair!

Is There A Limit To What I Can Create?

Absolutely not! You are free to create anything you want (web, mobile, desktop), as long as it's related to Bitcoin and the Blockchain. We will also bring you some hardware gadgets so that you can create some cool hardware hacks.

What Can I Win?

The Hackathon is not about winning, but rather about a learning opportunity to create application prototypes in short amount of time with amazing new technologies. The prizes for the winners with the best application will be announced during the event.

What Should I Bring?

You will have to bring part of your hacking equipment (laptop, smartphone, tablet etc.). Everything else will be provided for you, including some cool hardware gadgets to hack with.

What About Food And Beverages?

Soft drinks, coffee and pizza will be provided for free.

What If I'm Alone?

Not a problem at all! Many people will come to the Hackathon alone and you can perhaps team up with these people and work together on an application. You can also work and present your solution alone.
